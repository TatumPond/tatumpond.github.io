Hi, I'm Tatum

It's great to have you here! I'm a digital creator with proficiency in 2D art, animation, design, and direction, who will be graduating from Boise State University with a Bachelor of Science in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technology (GIMM) in May 2025.

Ever since I was little, I've been a creative; from spending hours at the kitchen table with my brother drawing Pokémon to using a mouse to draw in MS Paint on my family computer, art has always been a part of my life. I was self taught up until college, where I studied as an Illustration major for a year and continued my studies in GIMM.

I am currently the Art Director of GIMM Work's Student Development Team, and have been since January 2024. Through my time here, I've had the privilege of working alongside a fantastic group of creators on projects that are impactful, imaginative, and one-of-a-kind for folks across the country.

Having the opportunity to create and collaborate with other passionate and inspired minds has been such an honor, and I'm looking forward to seeing what new roads await me.
