"Mushroom Gorge" (In Progress)

Started November 2024 - Clip Studio Paint

Progress of a concept piece I'm working on for a client project for GIMM 480 (Senior Capstone). "IdaGem Login"

Created in June 2024 - Adobe Photoshop

Background image for a client project I worked on all summer in 2024 at GIMM Works. This was featured on the IdaGem Website, an interactive 4th grade curriculum aimed to teach students about Idaho's history. I drew the website's assets, like this one, and was in charge of designing the site's colors, fonts, and overall layout.


Rotoscopy Project

This consists of over 160 frames and took me about two days to complete. One of these days I'd love to continue working on it by adding color or a background.



Schrodinger's Cat - Interactive Comic End Animation/Timelapse

During my GIMM 250 class in the spring of 2023, I worked on a group project where we were tasked to create an interactive comic on a complex idea, such as quantum physics or how atoms work. Our group was given Schrodinger's Cat as our prompt, and I was in charge of designing all the characters and lining the storyboards of the comic. This clip in particular is a timelapse of the last animation that plays at the end of the comic that I created, which I'm pretty happy with. This project was super fun to work on, and I learned a lot doing it.
