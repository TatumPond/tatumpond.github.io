"Mushroom Gorge" (In Progress)

Started November 2024 - Clip Studio Paint

Progress of a concept piece I'm working on for a client project for GIMM 480 (Senior Capstone). This project is all about world building and creating a cohesive environment and story for a future game. My task is to create what the "mushroom gorge" would look like. I'm creating this piece deliberatly and am focusing on use of color and detail.


"The River"

Created December 2023 - CLip Studio Paint

Background image for a personal project I was working on in the fall of 2023. I ended up having to abandon the project due lost interest and time constraints, but it's still a piece I'm proud of.


"You"

Created July 2024 - Clip Studio Paint

Sketchy piece I created as a gift. Sketching is my favorite part of my process, as I enjoy how loose I can be with my lines and how forgiving it is, and taking sketches and making them very detailed is even better. I would love to render this at some point, but for now I'm happy with how it stands.


"Whoopsie Daisy Logo"

Created in December 2023 - Clip Studio Paint

Redesign of my logo I created back in 2019 for my mom's flower business, Whoopsie Daisy Floral. I wanted to update it with fresher lines, colors, and wording, and I gave it to her as a Christmas gift. It's featured on her instagram, buisness cards, and stock cards placed in her bouquets.


"Sharks"

Created October 2024 - Clip Studio Paint

Piece I created as a gift for a friend's birthday. I wanted to try some water techniques and a semi-lineless style, and I'm overall happy with the result. The sharks featured are a great white, hammerhead, whale shark, leopard shark, and nurse shark.


"IdaGem Background"

Created in July 2024 - Clip Studio Paint

Background image for a client project I worked on all summer in 2024 at GIMM Works. This was featured on the IdaGem Website, an interactive 4th grade curriculum funded by the Department of Homeland Security aimed to teach students about Idaho's history. I drew the website's assets, like this one, and was in charge of designing the site's colors, fonts, and overall layout.


The Bunny Who Lost His Tail - UX Project

Created Spring 2023 - Clip Studio Paint, Adobe XD

This project was done for GIMM 200, an animation and UX class. The final for the course was a UX design and wireframe project where we designed a potential app. My app and wireframe was on a book I wrote and illustrated when I was in 1st grade. I redrew and rewrote the story for this project, and it was so much fun to breathe new life into something creative I did as a kid .
