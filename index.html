Welcome!

My name is Tatum Pond, a fourth year student studying Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technology (GIMM) at Boise State University, as well as the Art Director for GIMM Works' Student Development Team.

Through my studies, I've learned a lot about various topics such as web development, 3D animation, UX design, game design and development, and much more. I have been working for GIMM Works since January of 2024, and have been honored to work with a talented team of students to create professional and meaningful products for clients both in and outside of the city of Boise. My experience on the team has allowed for me to improve my design, technical, public speaking, and leadership skills.

My main areas of skill are within 2D art, animation, and design, areas that I have been interested in and practicing since I was 12. Being able to study and work within a field where I can embrace my love and passion for art and create meaningful projects has been such a joy, and I'm looking forward to see where it takes me after I earn my degree in May.
