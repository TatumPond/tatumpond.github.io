Island Run - 2D Platformer

Island Run is a game that I began designing, developing, and directing during GIMM 110 in the fall of 2022. This game has since been worked on again in the fall of 2023, where I created a game trailer for it, as well as adding a day and night cycle. You play as a dog who's trying to escape a volcanic eruption on a tropical island, while you collect moths and try to reach the end of the level. This was the first time I ever attempted to code or develop any sort of game, so I hold it very near and dear to me. I learned so many new skills and it tested me so much, but I had a really good time making it.


Wonderful Wonderful - Fake "The Lion King" Trailer

This is a project I did for my GIMM 100 final in the fall of 2022, which was a class centered around 2D art and animation. I decided to do my project on The Lion King, one of my favorite Disney movies, and directed it to seem like a trailer for the movie. This was one of the first full projects I did in Clip Studio Paint, and at the time I was still very new to the program, so I learned a lot about what it can do. I really enjoy drawing stylized characters, so I had a great time drawing TLK in my style.


Rotoscopy Project

This was a rotoscopy animation I made in the fall of 2022 of my dog, Declan. This was done in Adobe Animate and was the first time I used the program. This consists of over 160 frames and took me about two days to complete. One of these days I'd love to continue working on it by adding color or a background.
