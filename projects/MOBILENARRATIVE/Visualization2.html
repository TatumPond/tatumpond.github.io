Visualization II - Sales


This visualization displays how many sales each Pokemon game made relative to the years they were published. This data is simplified to show the overall sales across all countries and continents that released these games, which includes North America, Japan, South Korea, Australia, Europe, Hong Kong, and Taiwan. It's important to note that Red and Green, Japanese Blue, and American Red and Blue are grouped together in their sales, while the others are grouped independently or with their sister game.
