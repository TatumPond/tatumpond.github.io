Visualization III - Consoles


This visualization was created by me using Clip Studio Paint. It shows all of the different consoles that Nintendo released their games on, as well as what games were on what consoles. They are in order from the earliest console (GameBoy) to the current console (Nintendo Switch). All of the original Generation I and II games are also on the 3D Virtual Console, which came out over 20 years after the release of Red and Green.
